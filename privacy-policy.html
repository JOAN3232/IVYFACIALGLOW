Customer policy

Privacy Policy

This page explains how IvyFacialGlow handles customer information when you browse, create an account, place an order, or contact the store.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, order details, account details, and messages you send to us.

How We Use It

Your information is used to process orders, confirm payments, deliver products, provide order updates, improve the store, and respond to customer support requests.

Payments

Bank transfer confirmation is handled by the store admin. Please do not share sensitive bank passwords, card PINs, or private banking login details with us.

Data Storage

Customer account, product, and order data may be stored using Supabase and browser storage so the website can run properly.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact IvyFacialGlow through the contact details provided on the website.

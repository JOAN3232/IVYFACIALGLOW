Store policy

Terms and Conditions

These terms guide purchases, payment confirmation, delivery, and use of the IvyFacialGlow website.

Orders

Orders are reviewed by the store admin. An order may remain pending until payment and product availability are confirmed.

Payment

Payments are made by bank transfer to the account shown at checkout. The admin must confirm receipt before the order continues for approval and delivery.

Product Availability

Products may become unavailable or out of stock. If that happens after payment, the store will contact the buyer about replacement, delay, or refund options.

Delivery

Delivery timelines depend on location, order volume, and confirmation status. Customers should provide correct phone and address details.

Cancellations

Pending orders may be cancelled before approval. After approval or delivery processing, cancellation depends on the store admin's review.
